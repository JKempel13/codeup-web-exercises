The New project

This is my attempt to start a new project after Codeup.

What I've learned since leaving Codeup....

First Thing Second Thing Third Thing Fourth Thing

Continue to learn

No matter what is going on in your life after you graduate, you can't stop learning. Just a little everyday will keep your tools sharp and ready to transition into the career of your choice.

Stay in touch

The people you graduated with are the ones that know exactly what you're going through. Keep in touch with them and they will help you grow on this new adventure.

Do your research

During this time when you're not constantly coding, this is the time when you really should do the research on the companies you want to work for. The more you know about the company, the better it is for you when you go in for an interview.

Do NOT give up

There are going to be times when you feel like you made the wrong choice. Be patient, and keep working on your craft. Everything will come together.
